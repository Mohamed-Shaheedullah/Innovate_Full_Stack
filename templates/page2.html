Larry Bird

boston city archivesView a copy of this license

Larry Bird was born on 7th December 1956, in Indiana. He was nicknamed 'Larry Legend'. He played the power forward / small forward positions for the Boston Celtics. He was not thought of as one of the games great athletes, but he was a great achiever, winning three NBA rings. In his own words, 'I thought the game out'. His initial rivalry with his contemporary Magic Johnson turned to friendship, cemented by a dinner at Magic's house prepared by Magic Johnson's mother.
