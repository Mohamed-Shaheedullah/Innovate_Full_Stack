Who are basketball's greates players? The world's top basketball league is known to be the NBA in the United States of America, so it follows that the best players will come from that league.

The NBA was founded in 1946 and there have been many great players through the generations. The game has changed sigificantly during that time. I am focussing on the modern age, since the 1980's. The entry system for the NBA usually involves college, although a few players such as LeBron James and Kobe Bryant bypassed the college phase and went straight into the NBA.

The three players pictured above are widely considered to be among the best that have ever played the game and are three of my personal favourites.
